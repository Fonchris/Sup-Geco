Frequently Asked Questions

General Information

Q: What is SUP GECO?

A: SUP GECO (Institut Supérieur de Gestion et d'Economie Appliquée de Bafoussam) is a higher education institution located in Bafoussam, Cameroon. It specializes in management and applied economics education.

Q: What is the full name of the institution?

A: The full name is "Higher Institute of Management and Applied Economics of Bafoussam (SUP GECO)."

Q: Who is the promoter of SUP GECO?

A: The promoter of SUP GECO is Emmanuel KUE.

Q: Where is SUP GECO located?

A: SUP GECO is located in Ndiandam, Bafoussam 1st District, Mifi Department, West Region of Cameroon.

Q: When was SUP GECO established?

A: SUP GECO was created by Ministerial Order No. 05/0011/MINESUP on January 5, 2005, and received its opening authorization on August 16, 2012 (Order No. 12/0371/MINESUP).

Accreditation Information

Q: Is SUP GECO an accredited institution?

A: Yes, SUP GECO is officially accredited by the Ministry of Higher Education in Cameroon.

Q: What are the official creation orders for SUP GECO?

A: The creation order is No. 05/0011/MINESUP of January 5, 2005.

Q: What is the opening authorization for SUP GECO?

A: The opening authorization is No. 12/0371/MINESUP of August 16, 2012.

Q: Is the institution recognized by the Ministry of Higher Education?

A: Yes, SUP GECO is officially recognized by the Ministry of Higher Education in Cameroon as evidenced by its creation orders and authorization.
