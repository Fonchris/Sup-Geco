About SUP GECO

Excellence in Business and Economic Education in Cameroon

The Institut Supérieur de Gestion et d'Economie Appliquée de Bafoussam (SUP GECO) is a leading higher education institution specializing in business management and applied economics. Located in Bafoussam, the capital city of the West Region of Cameroon, we are committed to providing quality education that prepares students to become future leaders in business and economic development.

Welcome from the Director

Dear prospective students, parents, and partners,

It is with great pleasure that I welcome you to the Institut Supérieur de Gestion et d'Economie Appliquée de Bafoussam (SUP GECO). Our institution stands as a beacon of academic excellence and professional development in the heart of Cameroon's West Region.

At SUP GECO, we believe in education that goes beyond theoretical knowledge. We are committed to nurturing well-rounded professionals equipped with practical skills, analytical thinking, and ethical values needed to excel in today's competitive global economy.

Our dedicated faculty, modern facilities, and innovative teaching methods create an environment where students can discover their potential and develop into future leaders. We invite you to explore what SUP GECO has to offer and join our vibrant academic community.

Sincerely,

Prof. Jean-Paul Mbarga
Director, SUP GECO

Our History

Tracing the journey of excellence and growth since our establishment.

2000

Foundation of SUP GECO with initial programs in Business Management and Accounting, enrolling its first class of 75 students.

2005

Introduction of new programs in Banking & Finance and Marketing. Campus expansion with the construction of the first dedicated academic building.

2010

Received official accreditation from the Ministry of Higher Education. Celebrated the graduation of the 1000th student.

2015

Launched Master's programs in Business Administration and Economic Policy. Established the Research Center for Economic Development.

2020

Completed major campus modernization project including digital classrooms and a state-of-the-art business simulation lab.

2025

Celebrating 25 years of excellence with over 5,000 alumni worldwide. Breaking ground on new campus expansion project and launching innovative programs in digital business and sustainable development.

Our Mission & Vision

Guiding principles that shape our approach to education and institutional development.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality education in business management and economics that prepares students to become competent professionals, innovative entrepreneurs, and responsible leaders.

We are committed to developing practical skills, analytical thinking, and ethical values through a combination of rigorous academic programs, experiential learning, and engagement with industry and community.

Our mission extends to contributing to economic development and social progress in Cameroon and across Africa through relevant research, knowledge dissemination, and community service.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leading center of excellence in business and economic education in Central Africa, known for:

  • Innovative teaching methodologies that integrate theory with practice
  • Impactful research that addresses regional economic challenges
  • Strong partnerships with industry and international academic institutions
  • Graduates who excel in their careers and contribute positively to society
  • A vibrant campus environment that fosters intellectual growth and personal development

By 2030, we aim to be among the top five business schools in Francophone Africa, with international recognition for our programs and research contributions.

Our Core Values

Fundamental principles that guide our actions and define our institutional culture.

Excellence

We pursue the highest standards in all aspects of our educational endeavors, from teaching and research to administrative services and campus facilities.

Innovation

We encourage creative thinking, embrace new ideas, and continuously improve our programs and processes to stay relevant in a rapidly changing world.

Integrity

Inclusivity

Social Responsibility

Lifelong Learning

We foster a culture of continuous learning and personal development among our students, faculty, and staff throughout their academic and professional journeys.

Our Campus

Modern facilities designed to enhance the learning experience and student life.

State-of-the-Art Facilities

The SUP GECO campus combines modern architecture with functional design to create an optimal environment for learning and academic collaboration. Our facilities include:

  • Modern lecture halls equipped with audiovisual technology
  • Computer labs with specialized business software
  • Business simulation center for practical training
  • Comprehensive library with digital resources
  • Student center with dining and recreational areas
  • Conference center for academic and professional events
  • Sports facilities including basketball court and fitness center

Located in a serene environment in Bafoussam, our campus provides an ideal setting for academic pursuit while remaining connected to the business community.
