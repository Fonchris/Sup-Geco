SUP GECO Establishes New Partnership with International Business School

Posted by Admin | Categories: Partnerships, International

The Institut Supérieur de Gestion et d'Economie Appliquée de Bafoussam (SUP GECO) is proud to announce a new strategic partnership with the Paris School of Business. This collaboration will enable student exchanges, joint research projects, and dual-degree programs starting from the next academic year.

The partnership was formalized during a ceremony attended by representatives from both institutions, government officials, and industry leaders. This alliance represents a significant step in SUP GECO's international outreach strategy.

New Master's Program in Digital Finance and Financial Technology Announced

Posted by Academic Office | Categories: Academic Programs, Technology

Responding to the evolving landscape of finance and technology, SUP GECO is launching a new Master's program in Digital Finance and Financial Technology for the 2025-2026 academic year. The program aims to equip students with skills in blockchain technology, digital currencies, algorithmic trading, and financial systems automation.

The curriculum has been developed in consultation with leading financial institutions and technology companies to ensure graduates are prepared for the demands of the rapidly changing financial sector.

MAR 2025

SUP GECO Researchers Secure Major Grant for Economic Development Study

Posted by Research Department | Categories: Research, Grants

A research team led by Prof. Jacqueline Nkengfack has been awarded a significant grant from the African Development Bank to conduct a comprehensive study on sustainable economic development strategies for West-Central African regions.

The three-year project will analyze economic policies, infrastructure development, and investment patterns across the region, with special focus on creating sustainable growth models that can withstand global economic fluctuations.

MAR 2025

Campus Expansion Project Breaks Ground: New Facilities Coming Soon

Posted by Administration | Categories: Campus Development, Facilities

SUP GECO has officially begun construction on its campus expansion project. The development includes a new state-of-the-art business innovation center, expanded library facilities, and modern student accommodation buildings.

The project, scheduled for completion by September 2026, will increase campus capacity by 40% and introduce cutting-edge learning spaces equipped with the latest educational technology. This expansion represents the largest infrastructure investment in the institution's history.
