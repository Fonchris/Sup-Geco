Frequently Asked Questions

What is SUP GECO?

SUP GECO (Institut Supérieur de Gestion et d'Economie Appliquée de Bafoussam) is a higher education institution specializing in management and applied economics education.

Is SUP GECO an accredited institution?

Yes, SUP GECO is officially accredited by the Ministry of Higher Education in Cameroon.

What degrees does SUP GECO offer?

SUP GECO offers BTS (Brevet de Technicien Supérieur) diplomas in various fields related to management and applied economics.

What are the admission requirements?

Admission typically requires a high school diploma, a completed application form, and relevant documentation. Please contact the administration for specifics.

What are the tuition fees at SUP GECO?

For current tuition information, please contact the administration office directly.

Does SUP GECO offer internship opportunities?

Yes, SUP GECO provides internship programs to help students gain practical experience in their fields.
