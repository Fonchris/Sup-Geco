BTS Programs (Brevet de Technicien Supérieur)

The Higher Institute of Management and Applied Economics of Bafoussam (SUP GECO) offers a variety of BTS programs in the commercial and management sector. Our BTS programs typically span two years and provide students with the technical knowledge and practical skills needed for professional success.

Available BTS Specializations

1. Comptabilité et Gestion (Accounting and Business Management)

This program prepares students for careers in accounting, financial reporting, business analysis, and management accounting. Students learn accounting principles, financial management, taxation, and business law.

2. Bureautique et Secrétariat (Office Automation and Secretarial)

This program offers two options:

  • Bilingual Office Secretarial
  • Executive Secretarial

Students develop skills in office management, communication, document processing, and administrative procedures.

3. Action Commerciale (Commercial Action)

Students learn about marketing, sales techniques, customer relationship management, and business negotiation. The program prepares graduates for roles in sales, marketing, and commercial development.

4. Commerce International (International Trade)

This program focuses on international business operations, import-export procedures, international marketing, and cross-cultural business communication. Graduates can pursue careers in global trade and international business relations.

5. Informatique de Gestion (Management Information Systems)

Students gain expertise in business software applications, database management, systems analysis, and IT project management. This program bridges information technology and business management.

6. Transport et Logistique (Transport and Logistics)

This program covers supply chain management, transportation systems, inventory control, and logistics planning. Graduates work in transportation companies, distribution centers, and logistics departments.

7. Banque et Finance (Banking and Finance)

Students learn about banking operations, financial markets, credit analysis, and investment management. The program prepares students for careers in banking institutions and financial services.

8. Assurance (Insurance)

This program focuses on insurance principles, risk assessment, policy underwriting, and claims processing. Graduates can work in insurance companies, brokerage firms, and risk management departments.

9. Industrie d'Habillement (Clothing Industry)

Students study fashion design, textile production, garment manufacturing, and fashion business management. This program prepares students for careers in the fashion and apparel industry.

10. Économie sociale et familiale (Social and Family Economics)

This program combines social sciences and practical management skills focused on improving quality of life for individuals, families, and communities. Graduates work in social services, community development, and family counseling.

DSEP Programs (Diplôme Supérieur d'Études Professionnelles)

SUP GECO also offers higher-level professional programs through the DSEP qualification in the following streams:

Stream 1: Business and Organization Management

Specialty: Human Resources

This program focuses on personnel management, recruitment, training and development, compensation, employee relations, and strategic HR planning.

Stream 2: Marketing, Commerce, Sales

Students develop advanced skills in market analysis, strategic marketing, digital marketing, sales management, and customer experience design. Graduates are prepared for leadership roles in marketing and sales departments.

Stream 3: Accounting Expertise

This program provides advanced training in financial accounting, auditing, financial statement analysis, and accounting standards. Graduates can pursue careers as accounting experts and consultants.

Stream 4: Finance Accounting

Students develop expertise in financial management, investment analysis, corporate finance, and financial reporting. This program prepares graduates for leadership positions in finance departments and financial institutions.

Special Program: BTS + CAPIET Double Diploma

SPECIAL: Entry to 2nd Year BTS or Double Diploma BTS + CAPIET (Free)

Students with a technical baccalaureate + 1st Year BTS can opt for a 2nd Year BTS + CAPIET double diploma (free) in one year instead of two years (partnership between SUP'GECO MANAGEMENT SCHOOL and ENIET-P of the WEST).

Conditions:

  • Completed 1st year of BTS
  • Successfully passed MINESEC entrance exam to ENIET-P of the WEST in August 2019

Benefits:

1. Time and Money Savings

Save a year of training and reduce the costs of a year of training + get a free CAPIET diploma worth approximately 500,000 FCFA.

2. Enhanced Career Opportunities

Possibility to present with a strong chance of success the Entrance Examination to the 3rd year of ENSET.

3. Public Service Eligibility

Possibility of being recruited at category B2 in the civil service or as a teacher in colleges or high schools.

Admission Requirements

Required Documents for Application:

  • An admission form to be collected at the office
  • A photocopy of birth certificate and required diploma
  • A photocopy of the National Identity Card (CNI)
  • 4 passport-sized (4x4) photos
  • 5,000 FCFA application processing fee
Note on Scholarships: Complete your scholarship application form for excellence or social character at the time of submitting your admission files at Sup G'Eco Management School-Bafoussam. Eligible students can receive up to 100,000 FCFA in post-baccalaureate study scholarships.
